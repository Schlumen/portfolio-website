About me

I began learning programming at the age of 16 at a student research center. Together with my friends, I participated in several projects for "Jugend forscht", a student and youth competition in the field of natural sciences and technology in Germany. With one particular project, we won the national prize for the best interdisciplinary work. In this project I was responsible for writing the software for a prototype of a process engineering plant.

After completing school, I pursued a Bachelor of Science in Security and Safety Engineering at the Furtwangen University. Following that, I worked at the Paul Scherrer Institute, the largest research institute in Switzerland, as a radiation protection engineer for two years. However, I eventually decided to shift my career towards the tech industry and turn my hobby of programming into my profession. I am currently enrolled in the Full-Stack Web Development course at CareerFoundry, and have discovered that this is exactly what I want to do. I find great pleasure in programming and creating websites.
